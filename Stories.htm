Stories 
 
Banner- The banner is for visitors have not seen Dwight before and 
to also make a statement. I wanted this banner to be the first thing 
any visitor sees. 
 
Navbar- The navbar is equipped to help new users easily navigate 
through the webpage to any point that peaks their interest. It is 
conveniently equipped with an anchor tag that rapidly navigates 
through the page to whichever point they choose. 
 
Background-Image- I chose this background image to display a 
more political side of Dwight. Those who know him are familiar with 
his history in dealing with pranks, but this image truly illustrates the 
political seriousness of Dwight. 
 
Call to Action- The call to action is to simply remind visitors when to 
vote, and who to vote for. 
 
About- The about section is to give users more information about 
Dwight. It provides users who know Dwight, and those who do not, a 
chance to learn more about who they are voting for. This section also 
provides the voters with Dwights previous experience. 
 
Support- The support page is to inform users what Dwight believes 
in. It lists the issues in which he finds important and feels need 
support.  
 
Images- The images linked under the support page are to give the 
user a visual representation of everything said in the About section. 
 
Contact- This section involves the user and is very important. This 
allows the user to interpret all of the information they have received, 
and commit to an online vote. 
 
Link Page- The link at the bottom of the page gives visitors a better 
chance to learn exponentially more about the candidate.
